Spaghetti Bolognese

Classic Italian Comfort

Enjoy the taste of Italy with this hearty Spaghetti Bolognese. A flavorful tomato and meat sauce, infused with aromatic herbs, is served over al dente spaghetti. This classic dish is a perfect balance of savory and satisfying, making it a family favorite. Garnish with grated Parmesan and fresh basil for an extra touch of authenticity. Buon appetito!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Italian Herbs
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Fresh Basil

Steps

  1. Saut√© onions and garlic in olive oil until golden.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in tomato sauce, add Italian herbs, and simmer.
  4. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  5. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the spaghetti.
  6. Garnish with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
  7. Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Bolognese!
