Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Creamy and Satisfying

Indulge in the velvety goodness of Chicken Alfredo Pasta. Succulent pieces of chicken are smothered in a rich Alfredo sauce made with cream, butter, and Parmesan cheese. Tossed with fettuccine noodles, this decadent dish is a comforting classic that never fails to impress. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and extra Parmesan for a touch of elegance.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Fettuccine Pasta
  • Heavy Cream
  • Butter
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta until al dente.
  2. Saut√© chicken in butter until fully cooked.
  3. Add minced garlic, heavy cream, and Parmesan to make Alfredo sauce.
  4. Toss cooked pasta and chicken in the Alfredo sauce.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan.
  7. Serve and savor the creamy goodness of Chicken Alfredo Pasta!
