Delicious French Onion Soup

Indulge your taste buds with the rich and comforting flavors of French Onion Soup. This classic French dish combines caramelized onions, savory beef broth, and a hint of aromatic herbs, topped with a layer of melted Gruyère cheese. The onions are slowly cooked to perfection, releasing their natural sweetness and creating a deep, flavorful base. Served with crusty baguette slices, this soul-warming soup is the epitome of comfort food, making it an ideal choice for a cozy evening meal or an elegant starter at your next dinner gathering. Bon appétit!

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Beef Broth
  • Gruyère Cheese
  • Baguette
  • Butter
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Sauté sliced onions in butter until caramelized.
  2. Pour in beef broth and add thyme, bay leaves for flavor.
  3. Simmer the broth and onions for a rich base.
  4. Toast baguette slices in the oven until golden.
  5. Ladle the soup into bowls, top with baguette slices.
  6. Sprinkle grated Gruyère cheese on top of each serving.
  7. Broil until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy the comforting French Onion Soup!
